# 🚀 Building Advanced Applications with ChatGPT

## 1. Big Idea
Beyond simple Q&A, ChatGPT can **handle multi-step tasks, maintain context, and perform advanced workflows**. Using smart prompt engineering, we can create applications that are more interactive, coherent, and productive.

---

## 2. Chaining Prompts for Depth 🔗
- Each prompt in a conversation **builds on the previous one**.  
- Example:
  1. Q: "What are the latest trends in renewable energy?"  
  2. Q: "How could these trends impact global energy policies?"  
- Benefit: **Creates a continuous narrative**, allowing deeper insights and richer discussions.

---

## 3. Sustaining Context Across Sessions 🧵
- Maintaining context is like **keeping a storyline going**.  
- Example:
  1. Session 1: "Outline the steps to start a small business."  
  2. Session 2: "Considering our previous outline, what legal considerations should we focus on next?"  
- Benefit: Produces **more coherent and relevant advice**.

---

## 4. Advanced Workflows ⚙️
- ChatGPT can handle **multi-step processes** through workflow prompts.  
- Example:  
  "Create a step-by-step guide for conducting market research, then draft a survey based on the guide."  
- Using **one-shot or few-shot learning** with workflows leads to **richer results**.

---

## 5. Persona-Problem-Solution Framework 👤💡
1. **Persona** – Define the role ChatGPT should adopt:  
   - Example: Advisor to Ray Dalio, expert problem solver, detailed prompt writer.  
2. **Problem** – Describe the problem clearly, using quotations to capture exact phrases.  
3. **Solution Structure** – Be hyper-specific to guide the response:  
   - Include **online resources**  
   - Specify **mental models and frameworks**  
   - Reference **books or other materials**  
   - Ask ChatGPT to **generate its own internal prompts** to solve the problem.  

- Benefit: Provides **structured, high-quality, and relevant solutions** while reducing cognitive load.

---

## 🔑 Key Takeaways
- **Chaining prompts** builds depth.  
- **Maintaining context** ensures coherence over multiple interactions.  
- **Workflows** enable handling of complex, layered tasks.  
- **Persona-problem-solution framework** combines instructions, persona, output format, context, and examples for maximum effectiveness.  

---

## 💡 One-Line Summary
👉 Advanced ChatGPT applications rely on **chaining prompts, preserving context, designing workflows, and using the persona-problem-solution framework** to solve complex real-world problems effectively.
