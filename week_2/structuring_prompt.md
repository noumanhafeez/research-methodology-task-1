# 🧠 Structuring a Prompt – Simple Guide

## 1. Big Idea
A prompt is like a **foundation of a building** 🏗️  
👉 If your prompt is strong → output will be strong  
👉 If your prompt is weak → output will be weak  

---

## 2. Quick Recap (3 Basics)

Good prompts always have:

### ✅ Clarity
Be clear, avoid confusion

### ✅ Specificity
Add details

### ✅ Open-endedness
Allow better and creative answers

---

## 3. 5 Key Elements of a Great Prompt

To make your prompt powerful, include these 👇

---

## 🔹 1. Instructions (What to do)

Tell clearly what you want.

❌ Bad:
“What is the monarch?”

✅ Good:
“What is the current monarch of Denmark?”

👉 Clear instructions = accurate answer

---

## 🔹 2. Persona (Give a Role)

Tell ChatGPT who it should act like.

❌ Bad:
“Write a story”

✅ Good:
“You are J.K. Rowling. Write a short story about magic”

👉 This improves:
- Style
- Tone
- Relevance

---

## 🔹 3. Output Format (How to respond)

Tell how you want the answer.

Examples:
- Summary
- Bullet points
- Checklist
- Table

✅ Example:
“Create a checklist to prepare for a data science job”

👉 This controls the structure of output

---

## 🔹 4. Context (Background Info)

Give extra information.

Example:
“Here is a podcast transcript. What does it say about breathing techniques?”

👉 Helps ChatGPT give more accurate answers

---

## 🔹 5. Examples (Show what you want)

Give sample input/output.

Example:
“Here is my email style: [example]  
Now write a reply in the same style”

👉 Best way to:
- Copy tone
- Personalize responses

---

## 4. Simple Formula for a Perfect Prompt

👉 Combine everything:

**[Persona] + [Instructions] + [Context] + [Examples] + [Output Format]**

---

## 5. Example of a Perfect Prompt

“You are a data scientist.  
I am preparing for interviews.  
Create a checklist for preparation.  
Format it in bullet points.”

---

## 🔑 Final Summary

- Good prompts = clear + specific + structured  
- Use 5 elements to improve quality  
- Guide ChatGPT like an assistant  

---

## 💡 One-Line Summary

👉 **A great prompt clearly tells ChatGPT what to do, how to do it, and how the answer should look.**
