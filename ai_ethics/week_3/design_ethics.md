# 🧩 AI Ethics by Design — Simple Guide

## 🔍 What is AI Ethics by Design?

- Think **ethics before deploying AI**  
- Focus on ethical impact **throughout the AI lifecycle**  
- Encourages proactive decision-making instead of reactive fixes  

---

## ✅ Seven Key Checkpoints

1. **Defining Objectives**  
   - Clearly state **what the AI aims to achieve**  
   - Example: LlamaFlix creating a tool to diagnose rare illnesses ethically  

2. **Stakeholder Engagement**  
   - Involve experts, potential users, and clients  
   - Gather broad feedback to identify **ethical pitfalls early**  

3. **Data Collection & Management**  
   - Use **representative data**  
   - Store and manage data **responsibly**  
   - Avoid **bias** from skewed datasets  

4. **Transparency in Design**  
   - Document processes clearly  
   - Make decision-making **explainable** using XAI techniques  

5. **Bias Evaluation**  
   - Continuously **check for biases** in AI outputs  
   - Ensure **fair treatment for all users**  

6. **Channels for Concerns**  
   - Allow users to **raise ethical or practical concerns**  
   - Helps maintain **trust and accountability**  

7. **Iteration & Evaluation**  
   - Continuously **review and improve AI systems**  
   - Adapt to new challenges and evolving societal norms  

---

## 🌟 Takeaways

- Ethics is **not a one-time task** — it’s ongoing  
- Combining **good objectives, the right people, and proper data** ensures responsible AI  
- Transparency, evaluation, and iteration are **crucial for trust and fairness**  

---

## 🧾 One-Line Summary
> **AI Ethics by Design integrates ethical principles at every stage, ensuring AI systems are fair, transparent, and continuously improved.**
